About Me

I'm Jay Romanes, a multimedia artist with over 8 years of experience creating compelling visuals across traditional and digital mediums. My journey began with pencil drawings for my school activities but quickly evolved to encompass digital tools that expanded my creative possibilities.

My work is driven by a deep fascination with the world of art and human experience, often exploring themes of connection, identity, and perception. Each project is an opportunity to push boundaries and create something that resonates on both aesthetic and emotional levels.
